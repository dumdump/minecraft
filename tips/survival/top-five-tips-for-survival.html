Top 5 Tips For Survival Mode

  1. Find a good world. Don't be afraid to quit your world if the landscape is not good. If you don't have a good landscape to begin with, you will spend so much more time trying to make it good.

  2. Make automatic farms. Automatic farms are essential because they provide resources without you doing anything. You will spend much less time tending to a non-auto farm if you don't build an automatic one.

  3. Use Armor. Armor is crucial, especially at night. You may think it is not worth investing in armor in your early days, but you will regret that choice later on.

  4. Stay Organized. Staying organized helps you work on bigger projects like farms, cities, and bases.

  5. Don't dig straight down. Digging straight down when you go to mine gives you an increased chance to dig into caves that have monsters in them, dungeons, lava, and ravines.
